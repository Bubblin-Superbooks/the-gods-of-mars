Their blood-streaked heads and shoulders testified to the cause of their wariness as well as to the swordsmanship of the green warrior whose glossy hide bore the same mute but eloquent witness to the ferocity of the attacks that he had so far withstood.

So weak was he from continued exertion and loss of blood that but for the supporting wall I doubt that he even could have stood erect.
